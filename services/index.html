Our Services

Quality earthworks and landscaping solutions for Peel homeowners and builders

🧱 Limestone Retaining Walls

Natural limestone is the material of choice for Perth retaining walls — it's hardwearing, locally sourced, and looks better with age. Whether you need a small garden wall to level a backyard or a major structural retaining wall for a sloping block, we build them to last.

We handle the complete job: excavation, drainage, wall construction and backfill. Every wall is built to engineering standards with proper geotech fabric and drainage behind the stone.

  • Garden retaining walls — any height
  • Structural retaining walls for sloping blocks
  • Curved and straight wall designs
  • Steps and staircases in limestone
  • Drainage behind all walls included
  • Council compliance where required

🚜 Bulk Earthworks

Site preparation is the most important step in any construction or landscaping project. Get it wrong and you'll be fixing problems for years. We have the equipment and experience to cut, fill, grade and level your site correctly from the start.

From single residential blocks to multi-lot subdivisions, we deliver accurate cuts and fills with proper compaction — ready for the next trade.

  • Site cuts and bulk filling
  • Block levelling and grading
  • Fill importation and spreading
  • Subgrade preparation for driveways and pads
  • Dam construction and repair
  • Road and access track building

💧 Drainage & Site Prep

Poor drainage destroys foundations, driveways and landscapes. We design and install practical drainage solutions that actually work in the Peel region's sandy soil conditions — from simple spoon drains to complex subsoil systems.

Correct site preparation also means understanding your soil type, fall angles and where water needs to go. We factor all of that in.

  • Spoon drains and french drains
  • Subsoil drainage systems
  • Soakwell installation
  • Stormwater connection and outlets
  • Benching and fall correction
  • Drainage plans for council submissions
