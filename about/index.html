About Us

Peel region's trusted name in earthworks and limestone retaining walls

Our Story

Coastal Limestone has been operating in and in and around the Peel for over 15 years. What started as a one-man bobcat operation has grown into a full-service earthworks and landscaping business — without losing the personal touch that got us here.

We take on everything from small residential retaining wall jobs to large-scale commercial site preparation. The same care, the same quality, every time.

We believe in doing the job properly — which means proper drainage behind every wall, proper compaction on every pad, and leaving every site clean when we finish. That approach has earned us repeat customers and referrals right across the Peel region.
